12.5.2025 - Game Data Sniffing (GDS) and Singularity

The mod will automatically disable GDS when the player enters a new Singularity or exits or leaves Singularity challenge.

This is because GDS reads and writes to localStorage heavily, which is fine otherwise, but results in stuttering and other issues if it is enabled when starting a new singularity.

After a few seconds, the mod will automatically re-enable GDS for the new Singularity.
